Design Exploration

Poster Design & Pitch


We need to look for data of interest on the data.qld.gov.au website, design an interactive website prototype based on the data, and design a poster to help your audience understand your webpage. The poster needs to include your design concept and your target audience, also need to include your simple interaction process and an ideal persona.



I chose Queensland's marine pollution data in recent years, and wanted to build an interactive website that can help my audience users (environmentalists, students studying marine environment, etc.) quickly find pollution on the map.



Data URL: Marine pollution - 2019 to 2020
